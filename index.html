Research Interests

Bayesian nonparametrics and data confidentiality. My research focuses on proposing, studying, and applying Bayesian nonParametric models for single/regression density estimation and hypothesis testing. In addition, I work with Professor Jerry Reiter on differential private verification measures and statistical methods for generating synthetic data using nonparametric models. My work has involved interdisciplinary efforts at the intersection of statistics, computer science, political science, environmental engineering, and psychometrics.
